## 🛠️ Key Takeaway

For **Sector 2 (IPPU)**, the activity data is almost exclusively measured in **Megagrams (Mg)**, which is equivalent to **Metric Tonnes**. 

When collecting data, ensure you specify if the amount refers to the **Raw Material consumed** or the **Final Product produced**, as emission factors in `pega` may vary based on this distinction.
